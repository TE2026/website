Fees and Registration Process

The fees for attending the entire conference are set out below. Units are Great British Pounds (£). Fees for full conference delegates include:

  • welcome reception
  • conference gala dinner
  • an industry networking reception
  • technical visits
  • fees for the ISTE and proceedings publication
  • daily lunches and refreshments
  • access to all talks and workshops
  • welcome and delegate pack

Full Conference Delegates

Category Fee
Standard conference attendance £800
Early bird discount (before June 7, 2024) £640
Student discount (50%) £400

Single Day Attendance (Industry Delegates)

No paper need be submitted to attend from industry (including policy, NGO and related non-academic sectors). These fees can be paid on the day (online) if space is available. Register ahead to secure a space.

Day Includes Fee
Day 1 Talks + Welcome Reception £120
Day 2 Industry Session + Networking Reception £100
Day 3 Talks (excludes gala dinner) £100

Additional Fees

  • Additional paper charge: £100 per paper (maximum 2 per delegate)
  • Additional gala dinner attendee (non-registered): £200
    Restricted spaces – please apply early

UCL Students and Staff

UCL students and staff may attend presentation and poster sessions free of charge depending on space availability. UCL proof of identity required.
Note: This does not apply to presenting a paper – the standard registration fees above apply for presenters.
