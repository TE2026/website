Overview: Submitting a paper to TE2026

Most attendees at TE2026 will submit a paper for presentation or poster at the conference.

Submission Stages

  1. Abstract submission – Deadline usually in late January. (Max 250 words. Use the template and guidance.)
  2. Full paper submission – Deadline usually in late March. (Max 10 pages / ~5000 words. Use the template and guide.)
  3. Camera-ready submission – Date TBC. Must strictly follow the template.
  4. Conference presentation/poster – 9–11 July 2024. Guidance forthcoming.
  5. Optional journal submission – Selected papers may be invited for academic journal special issues.

See the Guidance and Templates section for formatting instructions and templates.

Call for Abstracts

CALL FOR ABSTRACTS PROPOSALS

With the world facing intense challenges socially, politically, and environmentally, what is the role of engineering in helping communities? The theme of TE2026 is Engineering for Social Change.

We welcome abstracts exploring:

  • Innovative design and manufacturing for social and environmental good
  • Product design with broad societal benefit
  • Systems approaches, ethics, and justice in engineering
  • Engineering education, inclusive design, and sustainable construction
  • Collaborative and interdisciplinary methods

Traditional TE Topics Include:

  • Research & systems analysis
  • Product & process design
  • Decision support tools
  • Education, lifelong learning and skills
  • Risk and knowledge management
  • Collaboration, management & teamworking
  • Smart systems, IoT, and Industry 4.0/5.0
  • Megatrends and new methods

New Collaborations Invited From:

  • Engineering practice in industry & policy
  • Design research & interdisciplinarity
  • Engineering ethics & philosophy
  • Business practice with engineering
  • Engineering activism & justice
  • Engineering consultancy
  • Engineers in non-engineering organisations
  • Development engineering

Abstract submission deadline: 31 January 2024 29 February 2024
